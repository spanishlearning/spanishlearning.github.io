Private spanish classes

  • How many classes can I take per week?

    As many as you like. Students typically take 5 classes a week if combined with group classes.
    We recommend taking private classes in blocks of two teaching hours.

  • When can I start?

    Any day of the week, including Saturdays and Sundays.

  • Where will my classes take place?

    Wherever you want, be it at AIL Madrid, your office or in the comfort of your own home.

  • What's the timetable?

    Your private Spanish classes are scheduled at your convenience. They may take place any day of the week, including weekends, between 8:30 and 22:00.

  • What's the minimum age to take private classes?

    There are no age limits for individual classes. We organize private Spanish classes for children, teenagers and adults.

  • What levels are available?

    6 different levels, from complete beginners to fluency.

  • How long does each class last?

    50 minutes. Each session can consist of more than one class.

  • What's the minimum number of classes I can book?

    One.
